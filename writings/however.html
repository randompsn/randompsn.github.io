[物化]然而

Notes: This is a (WIP) fanfiction for anthropomoized Physics and Chemistry. I've been shipping them ever since 24 Spring.
I have no idea how to write settings or plots so I just wrote such nonsensical ramblings, aka how to avoid using third-person pronouns and writing footnotes longer than the main part. Roughly speaking it's from Physics's perspective. My knowledge of the two subjects are extremely unequal and there're lots of misused concepts & ambiguity just for the artistic effect, forgive me for being sloppy when expressing my immature opinions on them orz. Most discussions are really just rephrasing what the great people have said (mainly, that Bongo Drum player and that US team coach).


完全不会写设定剧情所以干脆摆烂写出来这堆呓语一样的东西又名如何花式避免第三人称代词以及把注脚写的比正文长，但粗略来说有点算是物的视角。高中生&两边知识储备极度不对等，有很多为了艺术效果服务的概念语义上混用滥用，对于学科发表的稚嫩意见中有不妥当的请见谅orz讨论的部分基本都是在复读伟人们说过的话（主要是神秘邦戈鼓手男子1 和神秘国集教练男子2）。
